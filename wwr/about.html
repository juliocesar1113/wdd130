Our History

Founded in 2005, We started with a single raft and a dream. Over two decades later, we are one of the leading adventure companies in the region that offers you the best safe experiences ever!

Rafting Castle Co. is more than just a rafting company we’re a team of passionate river guides, adventure enthusiasts, and nature lovers who are committed to creating unforgettable outdoor experiences. Since 2005, we’ve guided thousands of guests safely through some of the most thrilling white water rapids in the region.
